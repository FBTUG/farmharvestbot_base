須安裝gazebo_ros_pkgs
http://gazebosim.org/tutorials?tut=ros_installing&fbclid=IwAR2LlxwI3-s2LaOrcWPE-FSZYbAoyndSzWMt_WpyePqJ6j40pdA0O_w5Rbg

以rviz觀看harvestbot:
roslaunch harvestbot_description harvestbot_rviz.launch
在gazebo模擬時可看到camera跟laser scan訊號
這邊會自己讀urdf，跟gazebo模擬一起用時機器人定義會互相衝到造成rviz機器人沒跟gazebo一起動

以gazebo模擬harvestbot:
roslaunch harvestbot_gazebo harvestbot_world.launch

在gazebo模擬時可打開rqt發送topic控制harvestbot:
roslaunch harvestbot_control harvestbot_rqt.launch
